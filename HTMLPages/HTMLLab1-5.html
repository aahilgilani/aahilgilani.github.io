Almond Green Salad

Fresh and Crispy
Ingredients
1/2 pounds of bacon
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
3 tablespoons of honey
1/2 teaspoons dry hot mustard
1/2 teaspoons celery salt
1/2 teaspoons ground paprika
1/4 cup olive oil
1 head red leaf lettude torn into nite-size pieces
1 (15 ounces) can maderine oranges, drained
1 bunch green onions, diced
3/4 cup slivered almonds
Directions
1. In medium skillet over medium-heat, cook bacon until evenly browned. Drain cool, and crumble.
2. To make the dressing, thoroughly blend the vinegar, haney, dry mustard, celery salt, paprika, amd olive oil.
3. Place lettuce, oranges, green onions, bacon, and almond in serving bowl. Toss with dressing and serve.
